Social Beyond Compare

"Familiar faces around every corner"

Towers' single-block design is unique on campus. Having everybody living under one roof greatly enhances the social atmosphere, ensuring you'll see familiar faces around every corner. Towers is also one of only two halls on campus to provide catered meals 7 days a week, so you'll never go hungry!
