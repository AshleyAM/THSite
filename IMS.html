IMS

Intramural Sports

Towers fosters a competitive culture, and hence we excell in sport. Last year, we ranked fourth out of sixteen halls in the IMS tables. That's not to say, however, that we don't welcome less skilled players.

Anyone is welcome to turn up to any training session for any sport with an equal chance of being drafted for games. However, if you don't quite make it into the team, our sport secs will be more than happy to help you get inolved in Loughborough's 'MyLifestyle' sports, which are catered to allow individuals of all levels of skill to enjoy playing matches.

What do we Play?

A list of all available sports.

Football, basketball, utsal, rugby, netball, handball, cricket, swimming, aquathlon, softball, rock-it-ball, hockey, tennis, badminton, bowling, wheelchair basketball, table tennis, squash.

To get involved in any of these events, or the afforementioned MyLifestyle events, speak to any of your sport secs. Results,rankings and match reports can also be seen on the blog. Game times are available on the events calendar.
