Stash

Stay patriotic to your hall with comfortable & fashionable stash

Towers has its very own line of apparel, design by our very own stash rep - Susie Starr. From onesies to tracksuits, t-shirts to snapbacks and even shot glasses, there are endless ways to represent Towers in style.

On top of this, it's Susie's duty to bring Towers' freshers theme to life. She will be designing the logo for this year's freshers theme, as well as designing and getting the t-shirts made. Kitting out almost 200 freshers is no small task!
